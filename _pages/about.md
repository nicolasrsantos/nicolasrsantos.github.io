About Me
======
Hello there! I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, where I'm mentored by [Prof. Vagelis Papalexakis](https://www.cs.ucr.edu/~epapalex/). My current work focuses on developing machine learning methods to address challenges in precision agriculture.

Prior to UCR, I completed my PhD in Computer Science and Computational Mathematics at the University of São Paulo, under [Prof. Alneu de Andrade Lopes](https://sites.icmc.usp.br/alneu/) where I developed graph reduction methods aimed at improving the efficiency and scalability of graph-based learning systems.

**Research Interests:**
- Graph Machine Learning
- Data-Centric AI and Representation Learning
- Large-Scale Data and Model Compression
- Efficient and Scalable AI Systems
- AI for Precision Agriculture
